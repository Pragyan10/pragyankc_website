University of Texas at San Antonio

I am a PhD candidate in the Computer Science department at UTSA, advised by Dr. Mitra Bokaei Hosseini. My work lives at the intersection of software engineering, requirements engineering, and natural language processing — with a focus on improving how software requirements are elicited, analyzed, and traced across the development lifecycle.

pragyan.kc@utsa.edu LinkedIn GitHub CV

01 — Research Interests

My research aims to improve the quality and effectiveness of software requirements to better support the development of reliable and trustworthy systems. I leverage diverse requirement types — user scenarios, privacy policies, feature requests — to trace and connect software artifacts throughout the development lifecycle.
